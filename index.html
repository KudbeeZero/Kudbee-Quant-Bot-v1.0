The short version

What is Kudbee Quant?

Kudbee Quant is a quantitative trading research platform that combines real-time, confidence-scored trade signals, institutional-grade market analytics, and a no-code engine to build, backtest, and optimize trading strategies across crypto, equities, and forex.

In plain terms: it replaces gut-feel trading with evidence. You design a strategy visually, prove it against years of real market history, and then receive live alerts when your validated edge actually appears — with a clear, plain-English reason behind every call. You always stay in control of execution; Kudbee tells you what and why, never trades behind your back.

FAQ

Questions, answered.

What is Kudbee Quant?

Kudbee Quant is a quantitative trading research platform that combines real-time, confidence-scored trade signals, institutional-grade market analytics, and a no-code engine to build, backtest, and optimize trading strategies across crypto, equities, and forex.

Does Kudbee Quant place trades for me?

No. Kudbee focuses on signals, analytics, and backtesting. It surfaces high-conviction opportunities and helps you build and validate strategies — you stay fully in control of execution.

Do I need to know how to code?

Not at all. The strategy builder is fully visual and no-code. Power users can drop into advanced mode, but you can build and backtest a complete strategy without writing a single line of code.

What markets and assets are supported?

Kudbee is built to be market-agnostic, with coverage spanning cryptocurrencies, equities, and forex as we roll out early access. Waitlist members help shape the roadmap.

How does the backtesting work?

Kudbee replays years of tick-level market history in seconds, modeling fees, slippage, and funding. It supports walk-forward, out-of-sample, and Monte Carlo validation so you can judge a strategy honestly before risking capital.

How much does it cost?

Kudbee Quant is currently in private beta with an open early-access waitlist. Joining the waitlist is free, and waitlist members receive founding-member perks as access opens.

Is this financial advice?

No. Kudbee Quant is a research and analytics tool. Nothing on this site or in the product is financial advice. Trading involves substantial risk — always do your own research.
